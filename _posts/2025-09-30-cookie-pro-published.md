I'm thrilled to announce my first-author paper, "COOKIE-Pro: covalent inhibitor binding kinetics profiling on the proteome scale" is now published in *Nature Communications*!

This project was a fantastic journey into the intersection of three beautiful fields:

**🔬 Proteomics:** We developed an unbiased method to quantify how covalent inhibitors bind to proteins across the entire proteome. I'm especially proud of this work, as all the proteomics techniques were built from scratch here in Dr. Wang's lab.

**💊 Drug Discovery:** COOKIE-Pro is a powerful tool to help create safer and more potent covalent drugs by giving a clear picture of their on-target and off-target interactions, and empower the data-driven decision during covalent fragment screening workflow.

**📈 The Beauty of Math:** At its core, COOKIE-Pro uses a two-step fitting model to translate complex mass spectrometry data into precise kinetic parameters (k<sub>inact</sub> and K<sub>I</sub>). I still remember the excitement when my math derivation demonstrated this was a solvable problem! It's this model that allows us to quantitatively decouple intrinsic chemical reactivity from binding affinity at scale.
