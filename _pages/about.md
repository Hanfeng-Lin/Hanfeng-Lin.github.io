Hi, I am Hanfeng Lin — a Postdoctoral Associate at <a href="https://www.bcm.edu/">Baylor College of Medicine</a> in Houston, Texas.

I am a chemical biology researcher with a focus on drug design and screening. My research interests span:

* **Bifunctional molecules** — PROTACs and Molecular Glues for targeted protein degradation
* **Chemoproteomics** — proteome-wide profiling of covalent inhibitor binding kinetics (COOKIE-Pro)
* **Structure-based computational biology** — *in silico* modeling of ternary complexes, MD simulation, and FEP-guided lead optimization

I completed my Ph.D. in Chemical, Physical & Structural Biology at Baylor College of Medicine, where I was mentored by <a href="https://www.bcm.edu/people-search/jin-wang-26228">Prof. Jin Wang</a>. Prior to that, I received a First Class Honours BSc in Biochemistry and Pharmacology from the University of Strathclyde (Glasgow, UK) and a B.S. in Pharmacy from China Pharmaceutical University (Nanjing, China).

I enjoy communicating scientific concepts across disciplines, exchanging expertise, and thinking through problems from a reductionist perspective.

To learn more, visit my <a href="https://hanfeng-lin.github.io/cv/">CV page</a> or <a href="https://hanfeng-lin.github.io/files/Hanfeng_Lin's_CV.pdf">download my CV</a>.
